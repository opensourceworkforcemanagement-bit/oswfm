Document Overview

This requirements document contains module-level requirements for the Open Source Workforce Management System (OSWMS) version .1. It includes compliance, hiring workflows including Form I-9 handling, SOC 2 requirements across modules, API reference, change-tracking guidance, and a future enhancements section. Each requirement has a unique identifier to facilitate traceability and is intended as a living artifact for product, engineering, and compliance teams.

1. User Management

Overview: Identity, authentication, Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), profiles, org structure, integrations.

Functional Requirements

  • UM-FR-001 – Create new user accounts manually or via bulk import (CSV, API, integrations).
  • UM-FR-002 – Edit user information (name, contact, role, department, employment type) and manage lifecycle (deactivate/archive/delete subject to retention policy).
  • UM-FR-003 – RBAC with custom roles, role inheritance, and dynamic permission updates.
  • UM-FR-004 – SSO (OAuth2/SAML/OIDC), MFA (email/SMS/authenticator), password policies and reset flows.
  • UM-FR-005 – User profiles (photo, contacts, emergency contact, employment metadata) with profile versioning and audit trail for changes.
  • UM-FR-006 – Auto-generate employee IDs and usernames; provide search/sort/filter capabilities.
  • UM-FR-007 – Organizational structure (departments, locations, teams), multiple assignments, and org chart visualization.
  • UM-FR-008 – Notifications for account creation, role changes, and access requests.
  • UM-FR-009 – Full audit trail for user activity and administrative actions.
  • UM-FR-010 – Integrations with AD, OpenID REST/GraphQL APIs for CRUD and sync.

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • UM-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Ensure module design meets SOC 2 security and availability controls (logging, access controls, monitoring).
  • UM-NFR-002 – GDPR/CCPA privacy controls; encryption at rest/in transit.
  • UM-NFR-003 – Performance: support 10,000+ concurrent active users; user queries <200ms.
  • UM-NFR-004 – Scalability: horizontal scaling, multi-tenant capable.
  • UM-NFR-005 – Security: OWASP hardened.
  • UM-NFR-006 – Availability: 99.9% uptime for auth and user APIs.

Future Enhancements

  • UM-NFR-007 – Compliance: ISO 27001 support.
  • Biometric/Smart-card authentication; AI-driven role recommendations; delegated admin; Self-service improvements; adaptive access; Integrations Workday, HRIS.

2. Time & Attendance

Overview: Clocking methods, shift calculations, break & overtime rules, integrations to payroll.

Functional Requirements

  1. TA-FR-001 – Clock in/out via web, mobile, kiosk; capture timestamp, IP, device, location (GPS), and method metadata.
  2. TA-FR-002 – Biometric device integrations (fingerprint, facial recognition), QR/NFC attendance, and anti-spoofing controls.
  3. TA-FR-003 – GPS-based verification for mobile check-ins and additional anti-spoofing measures.
  4. TA-FR-004 – Shift definitions (fixed, flexible, rotating), automatic hours calculation, rounding rules, and grace periods.
  5. TA-FR-005 – Break tracking (meal/rest), overtime calculation, and alerts when overtime thresholds are exceeded.
  6. TA-FR-006 – Missed punch detection, manual edits with approval workflows.
  7. TA-FR-007 – Timesheet generation and export to payroll.
  8. TA-FR-008 – Integration with payroll and scheduling via APIs and webhooks for time data exchange.
  9. TA-FR-009 – Audit logs for all time events and retention per policy.

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • TA-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Ensure secure logging of time events, integrity checks, and access controls for time data.
  • TA-NFR-002 – Offline sync reliability and data reconciliation.
  • TA-NFR-003 – Performance: record attendance in real-time with <1s latency.
  • TA-NFR-004 – Scalability: support 50,000+ daily clock events.
  • TA-NFR-005 – Availability: 99.95% uptime; offline sync for mobile clients.

Future Enhancements

  • AI anomaly detection (buddy punching), wearable/voice check-ins, predictive overtime alerts, advanced anti-spoofing.

3. Scheduling & Shift Management

Overview: Shift templates, auto-scheduling, swap workflows, publish/notify and calendar integrations.

Functional Requirements

  • SCH-FR-001 – Shift templates with attributes (start/end, location, role requirements), recurring patterns, and rules enforcement.
  • SCH-FR-002 – Auto-scheduler using skills, availability, workload, and demand forecasting.
  • SCH-FR-003 – Drag-and-drop calendar view; prevent double-booking; bulk assignment; preview total hours before publish.
  • SCH-FR-004 – Swap requests and approvals with compliance checks.
  • SCH-FR-005 – Calendar integration (Google, Outlook, iCal) and publish/notify features.
  • SCH-FR-006 – Forecasting and optimization suggestions using historical data.

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • SCH-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Protect scheduling data, access segregation for editors and viewers.
  • SCH-NFR-002 – UI updates <200ms for 1,000+ employees.
  • SCH-NFR-003 – Multi-timezone support and 99.9% API uptime.

Future Enhancements

  • AI-powered auto-scheduler, gamified preference handling, workload balancing, mobile offline scheduling.

4. Leave & Absence Management

Overview: Leave types, accrual, approvals, calendar visibility, payroll sync.

Functional Requirements

  1. LV-FR-001 – Custom leave types and eligibility rules with carry-forward, expiry, and accrual rules.
  2. LV-FR-002 – Accrual engine (daily/monthly/annual), manual adjustments, and balance display.
  3. LV-FR-003 – Leave request workflows, multi-level approvals, and notifications.
  4. LV-FR-004 – Team leave calendar and conflict detection when approving.
  5. LV-FR-005 – Integration with payroll and scheduling for paid/unpaid leave handling.
  6. LV-FR-005 – Support for leave banks allowing employee to donate leave to each other.

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • LV-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Secure leave PII, role-based access to medical leave info, and encrypted storage for sensitive attachments.
  • LV-NFR-002 – Process leave requests in <1s; support 10,000+ employees.
  • LV-NFR-003 – Secure sensitive medical leave data with strict access controls.

Future Enhancements

  • AI leave forecasting, leave simulation tools, smart approvals, geo-aware validations.

5. Performance & Productivity Tracking

Overview: Goals, KPIs, reviews, continuous feedback, competency frameworks.

Functional Requirements

  • PF-FR-001 – SMART goals, hierarchical alignment, weights and progress tracking.
  • PF-FR-002 – KPI definitions, automated calculations, and integrations for productivity signals/data.
  • PF-FR-003 – Performance cycles with templates, self/manager/peer reviews, calibration, weighted scoring, and multi-reviewer support.
  • PF-FR-004 – Continuous feedback, recognition, and anonymous feedback options.
  • PF-FR-005 – Competency management and integration with LMS for learning paths.

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • PF-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Secure storage of sensitive review content, auditability, and access controls.
  • PF-NFR-002 – Real-time updates <1s; scale to 100k+ performance records per cycle.
  • PF-NFR-003 – GDPR compliance for review data.

Future Enhancements

  • AI-driven insights, natural-language feedback summarization, gamification.

6. Workforce Analytics & Reporting

Overview: ETL, dashboards, KPIs, predictive models, and BI integrations.

Functional Requirements

  • AN-FR-001 – Centralized data warehouse, ETL jobs, streaming support for real-time metrics.
  • AN-FR-002 – Dashboards (pre-built and custom) with drill-down, cross-filtering, custom visualizations, and exports.
  • AN-FR-003 – Standard KPIs, custom KPI formulas, and benchmarking.
  • AN-FR-004 – Predictive models for demand forecasting, attrition risk, and cost forecasting with prescriptive recommendations.
  • AN-FR-005 – Reporting engine with scheduled and ad-hoc exports (PDF/CSV/Excel/JSON).

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • AN-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Data governance, access logging, data masking for PII in analytics.
  • AN-NFR-002 – Dashboards render <2s for datasets ≤1M rows; scale to 100M+ records.
  • AN-NFR-003 – 99.95% availability; RBAC and data masking for sensitive fields.

Future Enhancements

  • Narrative analytics, NL query interfaces, anomaly detection, streaming dashboards, mobile analytics companion app.

7. Payroll & Compensation

Overview: Payroll runs, taxes, deductions, bank file generation, GL exports.

Functional Requirements

  1. PR-FR-001 – Support multiple pay frequencies, pay methods (direct deposit, check, pay card), and multi-currency payroll.
  2. PR-FR-002 – Earnings types including salary, hourly, contractor, bonuses, commissions, premiums.
  3. PR-FR-003 – Import approved timesheets; apply overtime, rounding, and jurisdiction rules; tax engine for statutory taxes, pre/post-tax deductions, garnishments, and compliance per jurisdiction.
  4. PR-FR-004 – Payroll runs with staged validation, approvals, holds, rollback, and off-cycle support.
  5. PR-FR-005 – ACH / bank file generation, bank reconciliation, and failed payment handling.
  6. PR-FR-006 – Employee self-service for paystubs, tax forms, bank updates and dispute workflows.
  7. PR-FR-007 – GL exports, tax reports, remittance summaries, and year-end forms generation.
  8. PR-FR-008 – Mileage tracking and reimbursement: employees submit claims with start/end locations, distance, date, and purpose; support multiple rate types (standard IRS rate, company custom rates, international rates); GPS verification for mobile submissions; integration with mapping services for distance calculation; approval workflows; tax treatment per jurisdiction.
  9. PR-FR-009 – Mileage reporting and compliance: track limits and caps per employee/period; generate mileage reports for tax compliance (e.g., IRS Form 2106); audit trail for all claims; alerts for unusual patterns or potential fraud.

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • PR-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Financial data protection, strict separation of duties, encrypted payroll exports and secure key management; ISO 27001 alignment.
  • PR-NFR-002 – Accuracy & auditability: immutable logs and validation to minimize errors.
  • PR-NFR-003 – Reliability: idempotent/resumable payroll processes and secure backups.

Future Enhancements

  • Integrated tax filing, payroll forecasting, earned wage access, fraud detection, global payout networks, pay equity analysis.

8. Hiring & Recruitment Workflow (HRW)

Overview: End-to-end hiring automation – job posting, resume parsing, review automation, interview scheduling, onboarding, and compliance documents (Form I-9).

Functional Requirements

  1. HRW-FR-001 – Post job openings to integrated platforms and internal careers site; track source analytics.
  2. HRW-FR-002 – Resume parsing (structured extraction), resume storage, and candidate profile creation.
  3. HRW-FR-003 – Automated resume review pipelines with configurable filters, AI-based candidate scoring, and shortlisting.
  4. HRW-FR-004 – Interview scheduling, multi-round feedback capture, and offer approval workflows.
  5. HRW-FR-005 – Form I-9 handling: collect I-9 documents, verification steps (Section 1 & 2 capture), secure storage, retention rules, and optional E-Verify integration.
  6. HRW-FR-006 – Onboarding automation: checklist, equipment provisioning, account provisioning (User Management sync).

Non-Functional & Compliance

  • HRW-NFR-001 – SOC 2: Ensure recruitment data (candidates, resumes, I-9s) is access-controlled, encrypted at rest/in transit, and logged for audits.
  • HRW-NFR-002 – Compliance with Form I-9 retention and E-Verify rules where applicable; GDPR data subject access controls for applicants.

Future Enhancements

  • AI candidate scoring, chatbot pre-screening, bias detection & fairness reporting, automated background checks, E-Verify automation, resume anonymization for unbiased review.

9. API Reference

API covering core modules. Each endpoint has a unique ID for traceability.

IDEndpointMethodPurpose
API-001/api/v1/usersGETList users (supports filters: department, role, status)
API-002/api/v1/usersPOSTCreate a user (body: name, email, role, dept)
API-003/api/v1/auth/loginPOSTAuthenticate user (SSO/OIDC tokens supported)
API-004/api/v1/time/clockPOSTRecord clock-in/out (body: userId, timestamp, method, gps)
API-005/api/v1/timesheetsGETRetrieve generated timesheets for payroll export
API-006/api/v1/schedulesPOSTCreate or publish a schedule (supports bulk assignment)
API-007/api/v1/leave/requestsPOSTSubmit leave request (body: userId, type, from, to)
API-008/api/v1/performance/goalsPOSTCreate personal/manager goals
API-009/api/v1/analytics/kpisGETFetch KPI metrics and trends (supports date range)
API-010/api/v1/payroll/runsPOSTInitiate payroll run (staged run, returns validation results)
API-011/api/v1/hrw/candidatesPOSTCreate candidate profile (parsed resume fields)
API-012/api/v1/hrw/i9POSTUpload I-9 documents and metadata (secure upload)
API-013/api/v1/webhooksPOSTReceive webhooks for events (timesheet approved, hire completed, payroll posted)
